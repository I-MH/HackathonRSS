README:

Data of energy consumption

From: 5-June-2023
To: 8-June-2025


Five datasets: CharlesCarter.csv, EngineeringBuilding.csv, Library.csv, PSC.csv, ScienceTech.csv. 

Each dataset represents a specific building.


In each dataset, there are five columns: 
series: series number and is unique
unit: unit of measurement (kWh)
time: time when measurement is taken
cumval: cumulative energy consumption (kWh). It starts at zero
val: Energy consumption within a 10-minute time window (in kWh).
